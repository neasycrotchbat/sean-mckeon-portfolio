ABOUT

I'm a multidisciplinary artist and motion designer based in Richardson, Texas, with 15+ years designing and animating for broadcast, brands, sports and live experiences — currently Senior Designer at NBCUniversal.

From 2D storytelling to full 3D generalist pipelines, I build work that reads instantly and holds up on the big screen. I'm now pushing deeper into real-time graphics, Unreal Engine and Houdini — bringing procedural and interactive tools into commercial motion design.
